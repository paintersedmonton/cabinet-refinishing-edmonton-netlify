About Us

Royal Painting Edmonton specializes in transforming kitchens through expert cabinet refinishing and painting services. With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we help homeowners achieve their dream kitchens without the high cost of full cabinet replacement.

Our Services

Cabinet Refinishing

Our professional cabinet refinishing service completely transforms your existing cabinets, giving them a fresh, modern look at a fraction of replacement costs. We carefully sand, prime, and apply multiple coats of high-quality finish to ensure long-lasting results. Our refinishing process includes:

  • Complete cleaning and degreasing of all surfaces
  • Professional sanding to prepare surfaces
  • Application of premium stains or paints
  • Optional glaze or distressing techniques
  • Final protective topcoat application

Cabinet Painting

Transform your kitchen with our professional cabinet painting service. We offer:

  • Over 50 designer colors to choose from
  • Multiple finish options (matte, satin, semi-gloss, high-gloss)
  • Two-tone cabinet designs
  • Specialty finishes like chalk paint or milk paint
  • Durable, washable finishes that stand up to daily use

Our painting process includes thorough preparation, multiple coats of paint, and proper curing for lasting results.

Hardware Replacement

Complete your cabinet transformation with our hardware replacement service. We offer:

  • Hundreds of modern handle and knob designs
  • Soft-close hinge installation
  • Custom hardware solutions
  • Professional installation
  • Hardware finishing services

We'll help you select the perfect hardware to complement your newly refinished cabinets.
